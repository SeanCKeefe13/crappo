  2. Roll Button

    By pressing the roll button, the player rolls all unlocked dice. Dice that score points are locked and wont be rolled again until all dice have scored or the turn ends. Points can be scored as follows:

    • 1 = 100 points
    • 5 = 50 points
    • 3 of the same dice value is 100 x dice face
    • 4 of the same dice value is 200 x dice face
    • 5 of the same dice value is 300 x dice face
    • 6 of the same dice value is 400 x dice face
    Next...

  3. Addtional scoring rules

    • Rolling 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6 = 5000 points
    • Rolling no scoring dice on your initial roll is CRAPPO -500 points
    • Rolling 3 or more 1's is 10 x points value that would be awarded (eg. 3 1's is 1000 points)
    Next...

  4. Stealing current remaining dice

    If the next players total score is greater than 5000 points, they may choose to steal the current players board and start their turn with the remaining dice. If the stealing player does not score on this roll, they do not get CRAPPO but they do not keep the stolen points and their turn ends.

    Next...

  5. Ending your turn

    There are 3 ways a turn is ended. If you do not score on your initial roll, you have rolled CRAPPO and loose points. If you choose to roll your remaining dice and score no points your turn ends and you gain no points from this turn. You may choose to end your turn, keeping all points earned this turn.
